the rite · 6–8 min

Four thresholds of transmission

A single-user passage. No audience — only you, the lamp, and the letters.

ACT 01 — ԴՌՆԵՐ Threshold

Complete darkness. A single virtual gantegh (church lamp) ignites. Your eyes — and the AI's — adjust to the void.

ACT 02 — ԱՆՑՈՒՄ Crossing

You step onto a bridge of glowing Armenian letters. Your physical speed is throttled and synced to a continuous musical drone — the dam of the duduk paces your body.

ACT 03 — ՎԿԱ Witness

An AI “Keeper of Memory” meets you mid-span and asks: who are you, and what do you carry across the bridge?

ACT 04 — ԱՐՁԱՆԱԳՐՈՒԹՅՈՒՆ Inscription & Continuity

You write your name and a chosen word. They crystallise into a permanent 3D khachkar that joins a glowing, shared field — the digital diaspora.
